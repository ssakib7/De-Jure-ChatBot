## About
De Jure Academy (ডি জুরি একাডেমি) is a Bangladesh law-education platform founded in **2021**,
based in **Dhaka**. It prepares students for the **Bangladesh Judicial Service (BJS)** exam and
the **Bangladesh Bar Council** enrollment (advocate) exam, through **online** and **offline
(campus)** live courses, plus published preparation books.

- Website: https://dejureacademy.com
- Facebook group: https://www.facebook.com/groups/dejureacademy
- YouTube: https://www.youtube.com/@dejureacademy
- CEO: Walid Rahman · Managing Partner: Adv. Rafiqul Islam
- Mentors include sitting judges/magistrates (e.g. Md. Monirul Islam – Metropolitan Magistrate;
  Rezaul Karim Badhon – Sr. Assistant Judge; Fahima Akther & Rubaiyat Shawon – Assistant Judges).
