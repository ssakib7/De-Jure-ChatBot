Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 July 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how De Jure Academy (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects and uses information when you contact us through the De Jure Academy Facebook Page and interact with our automated Messenger assistant. The assistant is an AI-powered auto-reply service that answers questions about our courses, prices, schedules, books, and enrollment.

By sending a message to our Facebook Page, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information we collect

We only collect information you provide by messaging our Page or that Facebook makes available to us as the Page owner:

DataWhy we collect it
Your name and Facebook / Messenger profile ID To identify the conversation and reply to the correct person.
The content of the messages you send To understand your question and generate a helpful reply.
Your phone number and name (only if you choose to share them) So a representative can contact you about enrollment, when you ask us to.
Basic conversation context To keep the chat coherent across messages within a conversation.

We do not collect payment card details, passwords, government IDs, or any sensitive personal data through the Messenger assistant.

3. How we use your information

  • To automatically reply to your questions about De Jure Academy.
  • To provide course, pricing, schedule, and enrollment information.
  • If you ask to be contacted, to pass your name and phone number to our admissions team for follow-up.
  • To improve the accuracy and helpfulness of our responses.

4. Service providers we share data with

To operate the assistant, limited data is processed by trusted third-party services. We do not sell your personal information to anyone.

ProviderWhat is sharedPurpose
Meta (Facebook / Messenger) Your messages and profile basics The messaging platform itself, governed by Meta’s own privacy policy.
Google (Gemini AI) The text of your messages To generate the assistant’s replies.
Google Sheets Name and phone number you choose to share To securely record enrollment leads for our team.
Telegram Name and phone number you choose to share To notify our admissions team of a new enrollment inquiry.

5. Data retention

We keep conversation context only as long as needed to provide a coherent chat experience. Contact details you share for follow-up are retained by our admissions team for as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and as required for our records. You may ask us to delete your information at any time (see Section 8).

6. Data security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. Access to enrollment leads is limited to authorized De Jure Academy staff. Connections to our services use encrypted (HTTPS) transport.

7. Children’s privacy

Our services are intended for prospective law students and adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us so we can remove it.

8. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information we hold about you, and you may opt out of being contacted. To make a request, reach us using the details below.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page reflects the latest version. Continued use of the Messenger assistant after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
